HOW TO PLAY

When you press PLAY a random word will be generated in the top left corner. Random squares will spawn. They each have one letter of the random word. You will have exactly 3 seconds to memorise the word and the letters on the sqaures before they disappear. After the letters disappear, you will have 4 seconds to click on the sqaures in an exact order so the letters behind it add up to the same random word.

Press P to pause
